Slick, from Madison, Wisconsin

Slick is a three-piece rock band that formed in Madison in spring 2024. Slick began as a solo project for Nate while he was playin in a band called Jaywalker. Slick grew to a full band later in 2024. The current lineup is Nate O. (g/v), Samantha S.(b), and Ian K.(d). They draw from the heydey of 80s college radio, classic punk rock and power pop singles, and garage punk revivalism. They plan on releasing an LP one day that won't be so lo-fi.
